📋 Business Registration & Tax Compliance

⚠️ Current status: First purchase of ₹43,910 made without GST bills. This is common for first-time visits — but going forward, GST compliance is mandatory for e-commerce.

Below is your complete step-by-step compliance roadmap.

🏢 Step-by-Step Business Setup

Week 1 — Business Structure

Recommended: Sole Proprietorship under Rohini's name — simplest, fastest, cheapest. Can upgrade to Pvt Ltd later under Misc Archive. Alternatively, register as LLP if both Anoop & Rohini want formal co-ownership.

Week 1–2 — GST Registration (FREE)

Portal: gst.gov.in → "Register Now"
Documents: PAN card (Rohini's) + Aadhaar card + Business address proof (electricity bill / rent agreement) + Bank passbook/statement + Photograph of proprietor
Process: Part A (TRN generation via OTP) → Part B (business details, bank info, upload docs) → Aadhaar verification → ARN issued → GSTIN in 3–7 working days
Cost: Completely FREE on the portal
Why mandatory: GST registration is required for e-commerce sellers AND interstate sales — both apply to 70YY. Also needed to list on Amazon/Flipkart later.
HSN Code: 7117 (Imitation jewelry) — 3% GST. If turnover under ₹1.5Cr, opt for Composition Scheme at 1%.

Week 2 — Trade License & Shop Act

Apply at Trivandrum Municipal Corporation or Kerala K-SMART portal online. Documents: ID proof, address proof, business nature declaration. Cost: ₹500–2,000. Processing: 1–2 weeks.

Week 2–3 — MSME / Udyam Registration (FREE)

Portal: udyamregistration.gov.in — completely free, self-declaration based. Classify as "Micro Enterprise" (investment under ₹1Cr). Benefits: easier bank loans, government scheme access, tax concessions, priority in tenders.

Week 3 — Dedicated Business Bank Account

Open a current account in business name. Recommended banks: HDFC, ICICI, Kotak (all integrate well with Razorpay/Cashfree). Separate personal and business finances from day one.

Week 3–4 — Payment Gateway

Apply for Razorpay or Cashfree. Needs: GSTIN, PAN, bank account, business proof. Setup: 2–3 days. Enable: UPI, cards, net banking, EMI, PayLater. Fee: ~2% per transaction.

Week 4 — Shipping Partner

Shiprocket (starts ₹26/500g, integrates with most ecom platforms) or Delhivery Direct. Set up COD option too — 40–50% of Indian ecom orders are still COD.

Month 2 — Trademark Filing

File at ipindia.gov.in for "70YY" name + logo. Cost: ₹4,500 for individuals/MSME. Full registration takes 18–24 months, but filing gives you ™ status immediately. Protect the brand early.

📋 Brand Story — For Website "About Us"

In a small house in Kerala, two sisters — Prasanna (70) and Ramani (70) — grew up surrounded by the shimmer of temple jewelry. For decades, they selected pieces for their daughters' weddings, their granddaughters' birthdays, their own temple visits.

One day, their daughter Rohini asked them a question: "Amma, what if you started your own business?"

They laughed. They're 70! What do they know about the internet? About "e-commerce"? About "Instagram"?

But then they thought — who better to pick jewelry than two women who've been wearing it for half a century?

And so, 70YY — 70 Years Young was born. Every piece you find here has been hand-picked in the wholesale markets of Chennai by Prasanna Amma and Ramani Aunty themselves. If it doesn't pass the Amma test — the touch, the weight, the way it catches light against a white saree — it doesn't make it to our store.

We're proof that dreams don't have an expiry date. Welcome to our family.
