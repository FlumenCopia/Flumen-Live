1,500–2,000 Instagram Followers (Month 3) Month 1: 200–500 followers. Month 2: 700–1,000. Month 3: 1,500–2,000. Organic + promoted growth. 5,000 is a 6–9 month target with consistent content. Realistic by Month 3

500–1,000 Monthly Website Visitors Month 1 (post rebuild): 200–400 visitors from ads + GMB. Month 3: 500–1,000. Organic search adds more from Month 4+ as SEO builds. Target: Month 2–3
4+ Reels Published / Month 1 Reel per week minimum. Kitchen transformations and product demos consistently perform best. Each Reel gets a 3–5 day boost budget of ₹500–1,000 if it performs well organically. Ongoing from Week 4
5–10 WhatsApp Enquiries / Month Tracked via WhatsApp click events in Meta and Google Ads. Strong WhatsApp CTAs in ads and Reels can generate 5–10 direct messages per month by Month 2. Target: Month 2
3–5 GMB Enquiries / Month Calls + direction clicks + website clicks from Google My Business. Modest but highly relevant — these are people searching specifically for kitchen equipment in the area. Target: Month 2
1 Project Closed from Digital Even 1 hotel kitchen project from digital = ₹5–15 Lakh revenue for Polemarks. That alone justifies the entire 3-month investment. Month 2–3 is a realistic first-close target. Target: Month 2–3
💬 Speed of follow-up is everything. Digital generates enquiries — converting them to sales is the Polemarks team's job. Every WhatsApp message and form submission should be followed up within 2 hours during business hours. No digital strategy can overcome slow response.
