Section 05

Gaps to Close. Wins to Scale.

A clear-eyed assessment of what is working and what is holding KACV back — with the strategic path for each.

Critical Gaps — Addressable Now

No Conversion Tracking (Pixel)

Meta cannot learn who is buying tickets versus who is just clicking. Without a Pixel, every ad is flying blind. The algorithm cannot optimise for buyers — it is optimising for curiosity. Installing the Pixel and setting up purchase events costs nothing but changes everything.

Wrong Campaign Objective

All three Holi campaigns ran on "Traffic" objective. For ticket sales, the objective must be "Conversions" or "Sales". Traffic campaigns bring visitors. Conversion campaigns bring buyers. This single change is estimated to improve cost-per-ticket by 3–5x.

Zero Tourist Geo-Targeting

There are currently no campaigns specifically targeting people who are physically in or near Kovalam right now. A ₹500/day campaign targeting people within 15km of Kovalam hotels — who are there as tourists, not locals — is one of the highest-conversion ad strategies possible for an attraction venue.

No Retargeting Infrastructure

10,204 people clicked the Holi ad. None of them are being shown a follow-up ad. Industry data: 70–80% of purchases need 3+ touchpoints. All of this warm traffic is being left behind.

Proven Strengths — Build on These

✓ Content formula is working

The 138K-view Reel (human face + Holi colours) proves the formula. Close-up authentic moments, event energy, artisan stories — this content type works. Systemise it: 3–4 Reels per week following the proven format.

✓ 16,651 shares in 30 days

This is an extraordinary organic sharing rate. It means the content is genuinely compelling and the algorithm is rewarding it. This is the foundation for a very efficient content-led growth strategy.

✓ Profile activity spiked 369%

8,302 profile actions including 1,470 external link taps shows that intent exists. People want to visit, book, and engage. The gap is simply converting that intent into action with better destination links and CTAs.

✓ Unmatched competitive position

There is no comparable venue in Kerala. This monopoly on the "authentic art village" category in the state is not being communicated in current marketing. Leading with "Kerala's only dedicated arts and crafts village" in every channel will convert curiosity into action.

Section 06

The Roadmap — 90 Days to Revenue

A practical, sequenced plan that prioritises the fastest revenue impact first, then builds the long-term growth foundation beneath it.

Phase 1 — Fix the Funnel (Week 1–2)

Before spending another rupee on ads, the conversion infrastructure must be in place. Otherwise the spend is wasted.

  • Install Meta Pixel + Google Tag Manager on kacvkovalam.org
  • Create dedicated event landing pages — not homepage links
  • Rewrite Instagram bio with clear booking call to action
  • Fully optimise Google My Business — photos, categories, booking button
  • Set up conversion tracking events: view content, add to cart, purchase

Phase 2 — Turn on the Tourist Funnel (Week 2–4)

The fastest-converting audience is tourists already in Kovalam. This campaign can be live in 48 hours and will show results within 7–10 days.

  • Launch 15km tourist geo-targeting campaign — ₹500–800/day
  • Launch retargeting campaign — target all past video viewers and website visitors
  • Switch Holi campaign type to Conversions with proper audience layers
  • Begin top 5 Kovalam hotel concierge outreach — brochures + commission offer
  • Contact 5 Kerala inbound tour operators — pitch group booking package

Phase 3 — Build the Engine (Month 2–3)

With the funnel working and tourist traffic beginning, focus shifts to building the sustainable monthly growth machine.

  • Systematise the weekly Reels calendar — 3–4 pieces/week minimum
  • Launch international audience campaign — UK, Germany, France pre-travel targeting
  • List KACV on GetYourGuide and Viator — free listings, international discovery
  • Begin monthly email newsletter to collected database
  • Google Search Ads — brand + tourist intent keywords
  • Monthly performance report — review and optimise all campaigns
