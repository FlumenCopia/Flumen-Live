Paid Advertising — 30% Management Fee

Meta (Facebook + Instagram) and Google campaign management. The ad spend is paid directly by KACV to the platforms — FlumenX charges 30% of the total monthly budget as the management and strategy fee.

Example: If KACV allocates ₹50,000/month to run ads — ₹50,000 goes directly to Meta/Google, and FlumenX charges ₹15,000 (30%) for campaign management, strategy, optimisation, and reporting.

What's Included in Ad Management

Campaign Architecture

  • Tour operator + corporate interest targeting

Management & Reporting
